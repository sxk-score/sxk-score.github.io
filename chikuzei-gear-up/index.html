Introduction:
Taiko no Tatsujin is good, and this music in it is nice!
So I attempted to arrange it for piano solo,
And with a MIDI Keyboard, it’s faster and more convenient!
Hope you will like.
(PS: You will have a better playback effect if you use Dorico to open the dorico file in the archive.)

Note:
Note: You need at least MuseScore 4.4 to open the MSCZ file. If you are using older versions of MuseScore or other notation software, MusicXML will be more suitable.
And you need Dorico 5.1 to open dorico file.
