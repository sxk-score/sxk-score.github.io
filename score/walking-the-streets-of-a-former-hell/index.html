Walking the Streets of a Former Hell

"Walking the Streets of a Former Hell" is the Stage 3 theme from the 11th Touhou Project work, "Touhou Chireiden ~ Subterranean Animism." Due to its use of the Chinese pentatonic scale and an arrangement that evokes a traditional New Year atmosphere, it is highly beloved by Chinese players, who often compare it to "Chinese Sausage."

INTRODUCTION

Happy New Year!
This piece is very suitable for the boisterous New Year, so I made it.
After all, ZUN described it as "a song composed with the image of the peaceful and boisterous streets of Edo."
However, it seems to have clashed with "Chinese Sausage," and now it has become "Chinese Sausage of Former Hell" lol.
But if you want to play it... then try to simplify the left-hand part as much as possible.
