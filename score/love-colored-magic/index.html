Love-Colored Magic / Love-Colored Master Spark

Love-Colored Magic first appeared in Touhou Fuumaroku: the Story of Eastern Wonderland as the theme song for the Stage 4 boss, Marisa. It later appeared in the Music Room of Touhou Kaikidan: Mystic Square, as well as in Touhou Suimusou: Immaterial and Missing Power as Marisa's daytime theme.
Love-Colored Master Spark is a remastered version of Love-Colored Magic, which first appeared in Touhou Eiyashou: Imperishable Night as the theme song for the Stage 4B boss, Marisa.
Both tracks are highly energetic, embodying the spirit of the "Strongest Magical Girl."

INTRODUCTION

The theme song of Kirisame Marisa.
I chose the version from Touhou 08: Imperishable Night because I believe it has a powerful momentum that showcases Marisa’s intense combat prowess.
During the arrangement, I used the official original MIDI files, which helped streamline the adaptation process, and my MIDI keyboard saw plenty of use. However, there were many repetitive modulated sections in the piece, which I simply copy-pasted.
If you plan to play it, let me warn you in advance: this piece’s difficulty is likely on par with Lunatic mode.
Finally, I’d like to thank everyone who has supported me throughout this project.

Note: You need at least MuseScore 4.4 to open the MSCZ file. If you are using older versions of MuseScore or other notation software, MusicXML will be more suitable.
