Maiden's Capriccio ~ Dream Battle

Maiden's Capriccio ~ Capriccio first appeared in Touhou Gensokyo ~ Lotus Land Story. as the theme song for the Stage 4 boss, Hakurei Reimu. It later appeared in the Music Room of Touhou Kaikidan: Mystic Square, as well as in Touhou Suimusou: Immaterial and Missing Power as Reimu's daytime theme.
Maiden's Capriccio ~ Dream Battle is a remastered version of Maiden's Capriccio ~ Capriccio, which first appeared in Touhou Eiyashou: Imperishable Night as the theme song for the Stage 4A boss, Reimu.

INTRODUCTION

Recently, I've been playing Touhou STG intensely, which has significantly reduced my sheet music production.
But it's okay—Touhou's music is still wonderful, like this one: Reimu Hakurei's theme song. I chose the version from Touhou Eiyashou because only this version captures Reimu's essence.
I used some official MIDI files and extend my gratitude to ZUN for creating such an amazing game and musical work.
Difficulty: LUNATIC
