Comforting Memories

"Comforting Memories" is a song composed by Kumi Tanioka that plays in the Overworld and in Creative mode. It is in Minecraft: Caves & Cliffs OST.

INTRODUCTION

This is one of my favourite pieces of music in Minecraft 1.18.
I made a full score and it has maybe up to 85 per cent restoration.
Hope you will like.
