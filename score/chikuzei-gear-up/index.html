Chikuzei -GEAR UP-

Chikuzei -GEAR UP- is an instrumental piece produced by Taiwan's Chai Found Music Workshop. Classified as a Namco Original in Taiko no Tatsujin, the track incorporates many traditional Chinese instruments and melodic progressions, creating a sense of building momentum.
